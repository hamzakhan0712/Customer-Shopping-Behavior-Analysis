## Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

### Project Overview
This project analyzes consumer shopping behavior to help a retail company improve sales, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty through data-driven decisions.

### Business Context
The company has observed changing purchase patterns across:
- Customer demographics
- Product categories
- Sales channels (online and offline)

Management wants to identify the key factors that influence buying decisions and repeat purchases, including:
- Discounts
- Product reviews
- Seasonal effects
- Payment preferences

### Problem Statement
How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?

### Objectives
- Discover customer behavior trends across segments
- Compare shopping patterns by category and channel
- Evaluate factors that drive purchases and repeat buying
- Provide actionable recommendations for marketing and product strategy
